About Yucando

Yucando.it is an online cloud task management system that lets you encourage your friends

Yucando offers a robust API for storing and accessing tasks as JSON documents. The philosophy is to allow the end user greater flexibility with their data, so that their workflow is not determined by their system, but their system is tailored to their workflow.

Yucando also offers a social element, where you can choose who sees what. You can share your todo list with a loved one, or show your advisor what you have on your academic plate. You can monitor progress in a friend's project and offer support when they are stuck. In short, allowing people to come together where day to day "busyness" divides.
